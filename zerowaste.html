ZERO WASTE

An awareness campaign on waste reduction has been asked by the association Zero Waste. This campaign includes three media, an awareness poster, bus shelter format, understandable by all, a brochure for public authorities with an educational issue and a set of cards of 7 families for children which must be fun but also educational. Zero waste is an association that defends the zero waste approach, it raises the issue of waste reduction, it intervenes at all levels, as much with citizens as with public authorities... An awareness campaign aims to change people's behavior and make them aware that this change must take place, without making them feel guilty. It must inform while bringing the solution to the problem.

In order to answer this problem, and to illustrate the fact of reducing its waste I decided to be interested in the numerical data these last ones because often they are massive and impressive. To make these data visible and visual seemed to me interesting I decided to expose the problem of the waste by the monstration of these data So I chose one data in particular, which seemed to me important, and especially speaking to all. 5 000 000 000 000 000 plastic bags consumed per year, it is the immensity of this number and the fact that we can easily relate to it, because almost the majority of consumers use plastic bags on a daily basis, and this is what makes this data according to me, particularly interesting

The brochure has almost the same layout throughout, with headings, important paragraphs, text and annotations on the left-hand page and graphs and tables on the right-hand page, The right page is the strongest page and the one we see first, the graphs, which translate the figures, allow us to visually understand the problem directly In order to add some colors, the titles and paragraphs in letter gothic are written in a dark green, reminding the organic dimension that brings the italic fauna The graphics are black and white, as simple and understandable as possible, the colors of zero waste are added when a differentiation is necessary within the graph or table The layout is not very colorful, denouncing the problems related to plastic waste, contrasts with the photos interspersed between some pages, photos of the Mediterranean Sea, they are colorful and without waste.
